ANT Company

The company is best known for its exposure to fish hatcheries (Marine and Sweet Water), Aqua culture by Bioflock Technology (Marine Ponds, sweet water ponds and pvc tanks) and manufacturing of fish feed supplements (organic).

The company manufactures 30 different types of fish feed supplements.

This is the only company in India using activated carbon, spirulina, fish meal (organic) and RA400.

These important ingredients that go into the production of fish meal and fish feed are in house products of the company. Which enables the company to market its quality products at a very reasonable and competitive rate.

The manufacturing facilities of the company are based on state of the art advanced American technology.
